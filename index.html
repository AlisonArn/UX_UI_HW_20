The Process

This project explains my process from start to finish. It shows how user research and analysis transformed into an effective and modern design.

Technologies used: InDesign, Figma

Timeline: Several weeks

User Research & Findings

I planned and conducted 5 interviews with current and potential users (young professionals) in Washington, D.C.

Problem Statement

The current website was difficult to navigate and visually unattractive. Users had a hard time finding the infomration they were looking for or used most often.
